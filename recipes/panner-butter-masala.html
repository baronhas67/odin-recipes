Panner Butter Masala

Image taken from ‘Roboflow Universe’

Description

Panner butter masala which is also known as “Butter Panner” is a North-Indian dish made with panner, spices, onions, butter and some cashews. It is a rich and creamy curry with a strong buttery flavour.

The cooking process begins with the sauteing of onions, ginger, garlic, tomatoes, etc.. Later all these are made into a creamy, flavour-rich liquid base. After this cubed panner is put into this base and with a bit of extra things,and voila your panner butter masala is complete!

Ingredients

  • Panner
  • Butter
  • Spices
  • Tomato pury
  • Oil
  • cashew(optionl)
  • Kasuri methi(optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 2 to 3 table spoons of oil.
  2. Add one spoon of Jira.
  3. Add some whole spices.
  4. Add one whole Onion cut into small pieces.
  5. Add one tea spoon of Ginger-Garlic paste.
  6. Add spice powder in appropriate amounts.
  7. Add tomato pury of two tomatoes.
  8. Fry this mixture for 2-3 minutes. (until it's raw smell is removed)
  9. Add 10 to 15 cashew's paste.
  10. Add half glass of hot water to the sauteing mixture.
  11. Add salt.
  12. Let it boil for a minute.
  13. Add fried panner.
  14. Let it boil for half a minute.
  15. Add Kasuri methi and corriander leaves and turn off the stove.
  16. Voila! your butter panner is ready. Enjoy it.
